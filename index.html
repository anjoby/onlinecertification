Online Issuance of Caste and Other Certificates

Welcome to the Revenue Department's portal for online issuance of caste and other certificates. Our online service allows citizens to apply for and receive important documents with ease, transparency, and efficiency.

Our Services

Caste Certificate

Apply for and obtain your caste certificate online. Our process ensures a quick turnaround with full transparency.

Income Certificate

Get your income certificate without the hassle of visiting government offices. Apply online and track your application in real time.

Birth/Death Certificate

Request birth or death certificates online. Secure and reliable issuance directly through our platform.

Real-Time Monitoring

Our advanced real-time monitoring system ensures that your application is processed swiftly. You can track the status of your certificate application at every step:

  • Application Submission
  • Verification
  • Approval/Denial
  • Certificate Issuance

We prioritize transparency and efficiency, ensuring that citizens have up-to-date information on their application status.
